My Top 3 Movies of all time

Megamind

I honestly believe Megamind is one of the best movies of all time. It's family friendly entails love and a fight between good and evil where evil turns to good to fight evil and wins! and its a really funny movie!

Kung Fu Panda

This movie is simply the best. I have no problem repeating this movie every day. It teaches discipline and hard work as well as other traditions the world has forgotten like simply respecting your master.

Yes Man by Jim Carrey

Jim Carrey is a Comedy Legend, Most energetic comic that trully left is mark on the entertainment industry and in this movie we get to experience his romance side. But overall a great movie that teaches alot about life when feeling stagnant.
